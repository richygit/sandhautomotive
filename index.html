S & H Automotive and Staples Automatic Transmission Service

S & H Automotive and Staples Automatic Transmission Service are located in Botany, NSW and have been operating since 1990. With over 20 years experience specialising in automatic transmissions we have a reputation for quality service at the right price. Having specialised in automatic transmissions for so long we are renowned all over Sydney for our skill and expertise in the service of automatic transmissions. Our services include:

  • All mechanical repairs
  • Taxi maintenance
  • Vehicle registration (Pink slips)
  • Specialist automatic transmission repair and service
We are your one stop for the maintenance and service of your vehicle. Having been in the industry for over 20 years if there is anything we cannot do we will know the best people who can, so please do not hesitate to contact us with any queries.
